Aranya Digital Services Ltd — Annual Report FY25 (Management Discussion & Analysis, excerpt)

Business overview
Aranya Digital Services is an India-headquartered IT and ITeS company providing application
development, cloud & infrastructure, data & AI, and digital engineering services. FY25 consolidated
revenue was ₹18,400 Cr (FY24: ₹16,900 Cr), up 8.9% YoY, with EBIT margin of 17.2% (FY24: 18.4%).

Revenue mix (FY25)
- By segment: BFSI 42%, Retail & CPG 22%, Healthcare & Life Sciences 17%, Digital & Data 12%,
  Infrastructure & Other 7%.
- By geography: India delivery serving US 51%, UK & Europe 19%, India domestic 18%, RoW 12%.
- Delivery model: ~73% offshore effort, 27% onshore.

Margin commentary
Margins compressed ~120 bps YoY driven by (a) elevated subcontractor costs during a demand spike in
BFSI, (b) higher cloud pass-through on AI workloads, and (c) wage revision effective Q2. Management has
initiated a structured cost-optimisation programme targeting subcontractor rationalisation, cloud cost
governance (FinOps), software licence rightsizing, and real-estate consolidation under hybrid work.

Strategic priorities FY26
- Protect EBIT margin while funding GenAI capability build.
- Improve billable utilisation toward 84% and reduce bench cost.
- Shift the pyramid toward a higher fresher mix and reduce onsite contractor dependence.
- Disciplined SG&A; consolidate the vendor base and renegotiate network and facilities contracts.

Headcount
Closing headcount ~84,000 (FY24: ~80,500). Attrition moderated to 13.8% (FY24: 17.1%).
