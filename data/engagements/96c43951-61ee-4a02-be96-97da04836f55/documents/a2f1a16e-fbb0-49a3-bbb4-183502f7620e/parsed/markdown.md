Aranya Digital Services Ltd — Procurement & Spend Control Policy (extract)

1) Competitive bidding
- Three competitive bids mandatory for any commitment above ₹1 Cr (USD ~120,000 equivalent).
- Single-source above ₹1 Cr requires CPO + category-head written justification.

2) Purchase orders
- No PO, no pay: all third-party spend above ₹2 Lakh must be on an approved PO.
- Off-PO / emergency spend permitted for production incidents but must be regularised within 30 days.
- Known off-PO leakage: cloud overages and short-notice onsite contractor extensions during BFSI peaks.

3) Vendor management
- Preferred-vendor panels per category; new vendors require risk + InfoSec assessment.
- MSME vendors: payment within 45 days per MSMED Act; flag related-party vendors for arm's-length review.

4) Change control / regulated workloads
- Switching security tooling or any vendor touching regulated client data requires CISO + Legal approval.
- HR/payroll platform changes require a 2-quarter transition plan.

5) Contracts
- Auto-renewals must be reviewed 90 days before notice deadlines; maintain the contract register.
- Early-exit penalties and minimum commitments must be modelled before any switch business case.

6) Commercial posture
- CPO objective FY26: reduce unmanaged/tail spend and consolidate the vendor base while protecting
  delivery continuity for clients.
