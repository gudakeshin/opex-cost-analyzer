Aranya Digital Services Ltd — Business Responsibility & Sustainability Report (BRSR) FY25, excerpt

Energy & emissions
- Campus electricity ~62% from renewable PPAs; target 90% renewable by FY28.
- Commitment: do not reduce renewable-energy sourcing to cut utility cost — green PPAs are protected
  spend even where grid power is cheaper.

Supplier responsibility
- 100% of material vendors covered by the Supplier Code of Conduct.
- MSME sourcing share target 8% of addressable procurement; MSME invoices paid within 45 days (MSMED Act).
- Diversity-owned vendor spend tracked; not to be cut purely for cost in FY26.

Workforce & community
- CSR spend at 2% of average net profit per Companies Act §135 — statutory, non-addressable.
- Skilling programmes (Coursera/Udemy/internal academy) treated as strategic investment, not discretionary cut.

Implications for cost analysis
- Carve out as non-addressable / protected: renewable PPA premium, statutory CSR, MSME-protected terms,
  and core skilling investment.
- ESG-linked commitments may cap savings on facilities (renewables) and certain supplier categories.
