Deep Research Brief — Indian diversified manufacturing cost (Uttam Industries)

Indian manufacturing EBITDA margins 12-18% for diversified players. Logistics 6-10% of revenue; energy 6-12% for energy-intensive segments. PLI schemes driving capex investment but also compliance overhead.

Peer observations: Logistics carrier rationalisation: 8-14% freight cost reduction. Solar/wind captive power: 15-25% energy cost reduction vs grid over 5 years. Maintenance inventory optimisation: 8-15% holding cost reduction. Subcontract rate harmonisation: 5-10% on machining and fabrication.

Constraints: IATF-16949 mandates qualified suppliers for auto. BIS certification limits supplier switching. Energy tariff structures (cross-subsidy) limit demand response. PLI scheme capex conditions must be maintained.

Addressable logistics, energy, and maintenance opex ~45-55% of total non-material opex.
