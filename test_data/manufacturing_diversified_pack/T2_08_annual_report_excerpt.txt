Uttam Industries Ltd — Annual Report FY25 MDA

Diversified manufacturer: Auto Components, Capital Goods, Specialty Chemicals, Consumer Durables, Engineering Services. FY25 revenue 35000 Cr (FY24: 32000 Cr), up 9.4%. EBITDA margin 14.2% (FY24: 15.8%). India domestic 72%; exports Germany UK US 28%. Headcount ~62000 (FY24: ~58000).

Margin: compressed 160bps from energy cost inflation and logistics rate increases. PLI scheme commitments driving capex. Initiating logistics optimisation and energy efficiency programme.

FY26: logistics cost to below 6.5% of revenue; energy cost reduction 8%; EBITDA margin recovery to 15.5%.
