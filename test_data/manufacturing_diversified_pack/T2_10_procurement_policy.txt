Uttam Industries Ltd — Procurement Policy

1) Three bids above 1 Cr for logistics, energy, and maintenance. OEM single-source for critical precision spares: CPO + Plant Head approval.
2) Auto components: only IATF-16949 certified suppliers in supply chain; quality cannot be compromised for cost.
3) BIS certification mandatory for products in regulated categories.
4) MSME subcontractors: payment within 45 days.
5) Energy: captive power priority; grid and PPA managed by Group Energy team.
6) FY26 target: logistics carrier reduction from 5 to 3; energy demand management to reduce peak tariff exposure.
