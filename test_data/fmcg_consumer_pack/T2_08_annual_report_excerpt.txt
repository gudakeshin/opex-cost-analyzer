Prakrit Consumer Brands Ltd — Annual Report FY25 MDA

Diversified FMCG: Foods & Beverages, Personal Care, Home Care, Baby & Health, Modern Trade. FY25 revenue 28000 Cr (FY24: 25800 Cr), up 8.5%. EBITDA margin 19.8% (FY24: 21.2%). India-dominant with presence in Bangladesh and Nepal. Headcount ~45000 (FY24: ~42500).

Margin: compressed 140bps from higher media costs and logistics inflation. A:S ratio at 13.2% vs peer median 11.8%. Initiating marketing efficiency and logistics optimisation programme.

FY26: A:S ratio to 12.5%; logistics cost below 8.5% of revenue; contract manufacturing to preferred-5 panel.
