Prakrit Consumer Brands Ltd — Procurement Policy

1) Three bids above 1 Cr for agencies, logistics, and co-packers.
2) Agency roster: maximum 3 creative agencies and 2 media agencies; roster review every 2 years.
3) FSSAI-approved ingredients and suppliers only for food categories; cannot switch for cost alone.
4) Co-packer quality audit mandatory before onboarding; annual re-audit for volumes above 50 Cr.
5) No PO no pay above 2 Lakh. MSME co-packers: payment within 45 days.
6) FY26 target: reduce logistics carriers from 12 to 5 preferred; e-comm fulfilment on rate card.
