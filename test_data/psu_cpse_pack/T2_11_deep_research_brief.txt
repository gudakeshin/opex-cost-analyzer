Deep Research Brief — Indian CPSE cost structure (National Engineering Corporation)

Navratna CPSEs: EBITDA margins 6-12% for manufacturing CPSEs; higher for service/trading. Subcontracting 30-45% of project cost. GeM portal procurement: 12-18% lower prices vs open tender for comparable items. DPE MoU framework mandates annual cost efficiency improvement targets.

Peer observations: GeM adoption from 30% to 60% indirect: 12-18% savings on indirect procurement. Steel vendor panel (4 approved mills): 8-14% over spot market. Energy captive power: 15-22% vs grid. Subcontracting rate discipline (DPE Value for Money): 5-10% reduction.

Constraints: GeM portal mandatory, limits discretion. CVC scrutiny limits single-tender use. Fixed-price contracts for government projects constrain cost pass-through. MSME/SC/ST reservation limits vendor pool.
