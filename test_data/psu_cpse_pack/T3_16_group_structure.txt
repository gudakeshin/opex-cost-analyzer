National Engineering Corporation Ltd — Group Structure

NECL-IN: India parent (Navratna CPSE; GoI 51.8% stake; NSE/BSE listed). ERP: SAP S4HANA.
NECL-EPC: NECL EPC Projects Ltd (India subsidiary; EPC arm for infrastructure). ERP: SAP ECC.
NECL-INTL: NECL International Ltd (India; overseas project execution vehicle). ERP: SAP S4HANA.

Entity tree: NECL-IN > NECL-EPC (100%), NECL-INTL (100%)

Governance: Board controlled by MoHI (line ministry). DPE MoU annual targets binding. CAG audits all subsidiaries. Related-party transactions require Audit Committee + Board approval per SEBI LODR. IC flagged related_party=yes.
