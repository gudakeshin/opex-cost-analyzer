National Engineering Corporation Ltd — Procurement Policy

1) All indirect procurement above 25 Lakh via GeM portal (mandatory per DPE guidelines and GFR 2017).
2) Open competitive tendering for direct materials above 10 Cr; Limited tender (approved vendors) for 1-10 Cr.
3) MSME preference: 25% procurement from MSME per government policy; payment within 45 days.
4) SC/ST entrepreneur preference per government MSMED policy.
5) Single-tender above 10 Cr: CMD approval required; CVC scrutiny applies.
6) FY26 target: GeM to 60% indirect; DGS&D rate contracts for standard items; reduce manual tendering.
