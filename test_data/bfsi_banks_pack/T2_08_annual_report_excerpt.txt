Indus Apex Bank Ltd — Annual Report FY25 MDA

Mid-size private sector SCB, Mumbai. FY25 total income 32000 Cr (FY24: 29400 Cr), up 8.8% YoY. Cost-to-income 42.8% (FY24: 41.2%). Retail Banking 40%, Corporate Banking 30%, Treasury 16%, Digital Banking 10%, Insurance Broking 4%. Headcount ~85000 (FY24: ~82000). Attrition 11.2%.

Margin commentary: C:I rose 160bps from accelerated tech investment and KYC/AML compliance staffing. Initiating ATM rationalisation and procurement discipline programme.

FY26 priorities: C:I below 43%; BC network replacing 200 sub-scale branches; digital txn share to 82%.
