Deep Research Brief — Indian hospitality cost structure (Surya Hospitality)

Indian hospitality sector recovering post-COVID; RevPAR growing 12-15% p.a. OTA commissions 15-22% of room revenue; direct booking share 25-35% for branded chains. F&B cost 32-38% of F&B revenue for luxury hotels. Energy 8-12% of total revenue.

Peer observations: Direct booking programme (loyalty + SEO): 3-6% OTA commission saving over 3 years. Central F&B procurement: 8-14% savings vs property-by-property. Building systems modernisation (BMS): 12-20% energy reduction. Housekeeping outsourcing: 10-18% cost reduction.

Constraints: OTA contract terms limit unilateral changes. FSSAI mandates approved food suppliers. Maldives/UAE properties subject to local labour laws. Seasonal demand variability makes staffing optimisation complex.

Addressable marketing, F&B, and maintenance opex ~55-65% of total non-staff opex.
