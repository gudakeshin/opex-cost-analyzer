Surya Hospitality & Resorts Ltd — Procurement Policy

1) Three bids above 25 Lakh for F&B, supplies, and maintenance. FSSAI-approved vendors only for food ingredients.
2) OTA agreements: Chief Revenue Officer + CPO approval for any commission rate change.
3) Building systems AMC: OEM or OEM-authorised only; minimum uptime SLA 95% in contract.
4) MSME local F&B vendors: payment within 45 days.
5) No PO no pay above 1 Lakh.
6) FY26 target: group F&B tender (single distributor per region); energy management system to control utility spend.
