Surya Hospitality & Resorts Ltd — Annual Report FY25 MDA

Premium hospitality: 28 hotels (18 India, 6 Maldives, 4 UAE), QSR chain (142 outlets), travel management. FY25 revenue 5200 Cr (FY24: 4600 Cr), up 13.0%. EBITDA margin 22.4% (FY24: 23.8%). Headcount ~22000 (FY24: ~20500).

Margin: compressed 140bps from OTA commission pressure and energy cost inflation. Initiating direct booking programme and energy efficiency investment.

FY26: direct booking share to 35%; OTA commission rate below 17%; EBITDA margin to 24%.
