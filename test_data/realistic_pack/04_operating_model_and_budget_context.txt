Northstar Retail Group - Operating Model and Budget Context

Business profile:
- Multi-country retail and e-commerce operator with centralized procurement and distributed BU ownership.
- Current cost pressure from margin compression and higher logistics volatility.

Organizational signals for analysis:
- Store expansion paused for 2 quarters; leadership expects SG&A discipline.
- Hiring limited to customer-facing and cyber-critical roles.
- Product and growth teams requested temporary flexibility for campaign spend in Q1.

Budget timing:
- Mid-year budget re-forecast freeze date: 2026-06-20.
- CFO asks for initiatives with visible year-1 impact and payback under 18 months where possible.

Known caveats:
- Some demand spikes in Q1 are seasonal and may normalize in Q2.
- IT and Marketing have overlapping tooling and agency spend that can create double-counting risk if not deduplicated.

What leadership wants from this cycle:
- Top categories with defensible savings potential.
- Root causes tied to practical levers.
- Phased view of savings (gross, cost-to-achieve, net) for executive review.
