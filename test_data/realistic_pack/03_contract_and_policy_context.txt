Northstar Retail Group - Contract and Policy Context (Q1 2026)

1) Contractual commitments:
- Microsoft enterprise agreement has a committed baseline through 2027-09.
- AWS committed spend program remains active through 2026-12.
- Regus lease lock-in for London and Manchester offices through 2028-03.
- Core legal retainer with DLA Piper reviewed annually; next break clause in 2027.

2) Procurement controls:
- Policy requires competitive bidding for commitments above USD 100,000 equivalent.
- Emergency purchases are allowed but must be regularized with procurement in 30 days.
- Off-PO spend has increased in digital marketing and selected IT categories during launch windows.

3) Compliance and risk:
- Security tooling vendors in regulated workloads require legal and CISO sign-off before supplier switch.
- Any change to payroll/HR platforms requires a 2-quarter transition plan.

4) Commercial posture:
- CFO target: EBITDA improvement and cash discipline in FY2026.
- CPO target: reduce unmanaged spend while maintaining service continuity for stores and e-commerce operations.
