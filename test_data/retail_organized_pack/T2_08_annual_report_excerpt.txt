Bharat Bazaar Retail Ltd — Annual Report FY25 MDA

Organised retail: Food & Grocery, General Merchandise, Fashion & Lifestyle, Electronics, E-Commerce. FY25 revenue 42000 Cr (FY24: 37800 Cr), up 11.1%. EBITDA margin 6.8% (FY24: 7.4%). Store count 482 (FY24: 440). Same-store sales growth 4.8%. E-comm GMV 5200 Cr, up 28.4%.

Margin compression: lease costs up 12% (new stores), logistics costs up 18% (e-comm mix), partially offset by private label gross margin uplift. Gross margin 22.4%.

FY26: private label to 28% penetration; lease renegotiation on 60 more stores; e-comm logistics redesign; 40 new stores.
