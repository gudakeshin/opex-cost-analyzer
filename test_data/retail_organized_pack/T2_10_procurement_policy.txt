Bharat Bazaar Retail Ltd — Procurement Policy

1) Branded FMCG: annual vendor meet; top-3 vendor bidding for shelf space; payment 30-45 days from GRN.
2) Private label sourcing: RFQ among approved manufacturers; 3-year contracts; category manager approval above 25 Cr.
3) Lease: Head of Real Estate approval; 5-year minimum; break clause mandatory above 5000 sq ft.
4) Logistics: 3PL panel (Delhivery, Blue Dart, DTDC); rate card review semi-annual.
5) MSME preference: fresh produce and regional staples prioritised from MSME suppliers.
6) FY26 target: private label vendor consolidation; logistics panel to 2 primary partners.
