Deep Research Brief — Indian organised retail cost structure (Bharat Bazaar Retail)

Organised retail EBITDA margins: 6-10% for full-line retailers; grocery-heavy formats at lower end. Lease as % revenue: 7-10% for organised retail. Logistics: 4-6% of GMV for e-comm; 2-3% for store. Private label gross margin premium: 8-14pp over branded equivalents.

Peer observations: Lease renegotiation on underperforming stores: 8-15% rent reduction. Logistics hub-spoke redesign: 12-18% per-order cost reduction. Private label penetration 25-30%: 200-350bps gross margin uplift. Vendor consolidation (FMCG): 5-8% on logistics & admin. Shrinkage programmes: 0.2-0.4pp improvement.

Constraints: Ind AS 116 — lease costs on balance sheet but cash savings from break clause activation are real. Minimum wage increases inflate store staff costs. E-comm logistics margin structurally lower than store.
