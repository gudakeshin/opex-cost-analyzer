Zenith Pharma Ltd — Annual Report FY25 MDA

Formulations (India + exports), API, CDMO, Medical Devices. FY25 revenue 12000 Cr (FY24: 10800 Cr), up 11.1%. EBITDA margin 22.4% (FY24: 24.2%). US market 30%; India 40%; EU 12%; RoW 18%. Headcount ~28000 (FY24: ~26000).

Margin: compressed 180bps from API cost inflation and field force expansion for new launches. US ANDA pipeline healthy (28 filings FY25). Initiating API dual-source strategy and CRO rationalisation.

FY26: API cost as % COGS to below 40%; field force productivity to 9+ calls/day; EBITDA margin recovery to 23.5%.
