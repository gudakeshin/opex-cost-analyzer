Deep Research Brief — Indian conglomerate cost structure (Bharat Ventures Group)

Shared-services and centre-led procurement are primary structural levers. ERP consolidation: 15-25% licence+support reduction. Group insurance consolidation: 8-15% savings. Legal panel rationalisation: 8-15%. Shared-services to Tier-2 cities: 20-35% cost reduction vs entity-by-entity.

Constraints: SEBI RPT rules limit pace of cost recharges to subs. Singapore TP at arm's-length. CCI scrutiny if dominant procurement position.

Addressable corporate OpEx ~60-70% of non-BU overhead.
