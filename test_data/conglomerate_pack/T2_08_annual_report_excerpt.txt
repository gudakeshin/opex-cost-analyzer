Bharat Ventures Group Ltd — Annual Report FY25 MDA

Diversified conglomerate: Chemicals, Infrastructure, Consumer Goods, Financial Services, Media & Retail. FY25 consolidated revenue 45000 Cr (FY24: 41500 Cr), up 8.4%. EBITDA margin 18.2% (FY24: 19.1%). India ~82%; Singapore UK RoW 18%. Headcount ~120000; corporate/shared-services ~8400.

FY26: complete Pune SSC; rationalise to 30 core tech vendors from 78; protect EBITDA at 18.5%+.
