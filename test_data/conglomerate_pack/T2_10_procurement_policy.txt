Bharat Ventures Group Ltd — Group Procurement Policy

1) Three bids for group commitments above 3 Cr; BU above 1 Cr or Group CPO waiver.
2) No PO no pay above 2 Lakh. Emergency above 25 Lakh: CFO approval 48 hours.
3) Intercompany recharges at arm's-length rates per Group TP policy; Group Legal+Tax approval for new IC services.
4) Group preferred panels: IT, legal, audit, insurance, travel, FM. New vendors above 5 Cr: CPO+CISO assessment.
5) Auto-renewals 90 days before deadline.
6) FY26 target: 15% reduction in tail spend (vendors < 1 Cr).
