Aranya Digital Services Ltd — Material Contracts Summary (commitments above ₹1 Cr), FY25

1) Microsoft (Enterprise Agreement — M365 E5 + Azure)
   - Term: 3 years to 2027-09-30; committed Azure consumption baseline ₹160 Cr/yr.
   - Renewal: 90-day notice; no early-exit penalty on licences, Azure committed-use locked.
   - Note: M365 E5 downgrade to E3 blocked by dependency on Defender + Purview in regulated client workloads.

2) Amazon Web Services (Private Pricing / EDP)
   - Term: to 2026-12-31; committed spend ₹280 Cr cumulative with tiered discounts.
   - Early exit penalty 15% of unfulfilled commitment. Shortfall risk if client workloads migrate off-AWS.

3) SAP (S/4HANA internal ERP)
   - Term: to 2028-03-31; min commitment ₹120 Cr; 180-day renewal notice; 25% exit penalty.
   - Considered locked / non-addressable for FY25.

4) Embassy Office Parks (Bangalore SEZ campus lease)
   - Term: to 2030-03-31; Ind AS 116 operating lease; ₹720 Cr min commitment over term.
   - 365-day notice; 40% early-exit penalty. SEZ status ties tax benefits to continued occupancy.

5) DLF Cyber City (Gurugram delivery centre lease)
   - Term: to 2028-03-31; ₹410 Cr commitment; 180-day notice; 30% exit penalty.
   - One floor (≈ ₹60 Cr/yr) identified as surplus post-hybrid-work; sublease under review.

6) Quess Corp / Allegis Group (subcontractor framework agreements)
   - Quess: India rate-card framework to 2025-09-30, 30-day notice, no penalty — renegotiable.
   - Allegis: US onsite staffing, rolling, 30-day notice — primary lever for rate harmonisation.

7) Bharti Airtel & Tata Communications (network)
   - Airtel MPLS to 2025-12-31, ₹72 Cr commitment. Tata Comms SD-WAN flagged at_risk (expiry 2025-08-31)
     — consolidation-to-single-carrier opportunity.

Addressability note: Items 1 (Azure committed), 2, 3, 4 are substantially locked for FY25 and should be
