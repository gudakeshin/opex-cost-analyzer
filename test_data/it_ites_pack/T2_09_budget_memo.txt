Aranya Digital Services Ltd — FY26 Cost Optimisation Mandate (CFO memo)

To: Delivery, Infrastructure, Procurement and Corporate leadership
From: Group CFO
Re: FY26 OpEx discipline and margin recovery

Context
FY25 EBIT margin fell to 17.2%. The Board has approved a margin-recovery target of +150 bps for FY26.
We expect roughly half from utilisation/pyramid actions (people) and half from third-party cost-out.

Targets and guardrails
- Third-party (non-people) OpEx reduction target: 6–9% of addressable spend, net of cost-to-achieve.
- Hurdle rate: initiatives must show payback under 18 months and positive NPV at WACC 13.5%.
- Effective tax rate for business cases: 25.17% (SEZ-blended).
- Prioritise run-rate (recurring) savings over one-time.

Timing
- Budget re-forecast freeze: 2026-06-19.
- Initiatives with visible H1 FY26 impact preferred.

Constraints (read with the contract register and procurement policy)
- Do not assume savings on contractually locked spend (SAP, Azure committed, SEZ lease) for FY26.
- Hiring limited to billable and security-critical roles; bench reduction is the lever, not backfill.
- Any vendor switch in regulated client workloads needs CISO + Legal sign-off.

What leadership wants
- Top categories with defensible, evidenced savings.
- Root causes tied to practical levers.
- Phased gross / cost-to-achieve / net savings view for the board.
