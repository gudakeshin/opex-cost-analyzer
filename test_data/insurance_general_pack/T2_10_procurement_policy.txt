Pragati General Insurance Ltd — Procurement Policy

1) Three bids above 50 Lakh. IRDAI IT security guidelines mandatory for all tech vendors.
2) TPA empanelment: IRDAI-approved TPAs only for health claims; no unapproved TPA above 1 Cr.
3) Reinsurance: all treaty terms require Appointed Actuary sign-off before IRDAI filing.
4) MSME vendors: 45-day payment.
5) Aggregator agreements: Chief Distribution Officer + CPO approval.
6) FY26 target: claims vendor consolidation from 12 to 5; digital claims app to reduce physical survey costs.
