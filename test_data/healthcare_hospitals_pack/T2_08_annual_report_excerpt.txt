Aarogya Health Network Ltd — Annual Report FY25 MDA

Multi-speciality hospital chain: 18 hospitals, 4800 beds; diagnostics, pharmacy, homecare, telemedicine verticals. FY25 revenue 8500 Cr (FY24: 7600 Cr), up 11.8%. EBITDA margin 18.4% (FY24: 19.8%). Headcount ~38000 (FY24: ~35000).

Margin: compressed 140bps from consumable cost inflation and contract nursing shortage. Material cost as % of revenue at 38.2% (peer median 35-37%). Initiating group procurement transformation and generic substitution programme.

FY26: material cost to below 36% of revenue; biomedical equipment uptime to 98%; bed occupancy to 75%.
