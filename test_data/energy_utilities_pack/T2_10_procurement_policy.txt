Sahyadri Power & Utilities Ltd — Procurement Policy

1) Three bids above 1 Cr O&M or 50 Lakh consumables. OEM single-source for proprietary spares: CPO approval.
2) No PO no pay above 1 Lakh. Emergency plant availability: CPO notification 24 hours; regularise 7 days.
3) Safety/PPE: approved-vendor list only; deviation needs GM-Safety + CPO.
4) BIS certification mandatory for electrical equipment; ISI mark verified at goods receipt.
5) O&M contracts: auto-renewal review 120 days before notice deadline.
6) FY26: OEM rate-card renegotiation for non-captive spares; inventory pooling across sister plants.
