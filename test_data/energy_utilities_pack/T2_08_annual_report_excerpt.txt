Sahyadri Power & Utilities Ltd — Annual Report FY25 MDA

Maharashtra-based integrated power company: thermal, gas, renewable generation, inter-state transmission, urban distribution. FY25 consolidated revenue 22000 Cr (FY24: 20200 Cr), up 8.9%. EBITDA margin 28.4% (FY24: 29.8%). All India-domestic revenue. Headcount ~18000 (FY24: ~17200).

Margin: compressed 140bps from higher O&M on ageing thermal assets and grid automation investment. Initiating plant O&M efficiency and SCADA modernisation programme.

FY26: O&M cost per MW below 175 Lakhs (FY25: 184); complete SCADA consolidation; expand renewable to 2000 MW.
