Meridian Global Services India Pvt Ltd — Annual Report FY25 (abbreviated; captive GCC)

India GCC of Meridian Global Inc (US parent; Fortune 500, Technology sector). Operates Technology CoE, Finance & Accounting SSC, Analytics & Data CoE, HR Shared Services, and Operations CoE. FY25 total cost base 6800 Cr (FY24: 6100 Cr), up 11.5%. Headcount ~32000 (FY24: ~29500).

Cost commentary: Growth driven by talent cost inflation (12% average salary increase) and campus expansion (new Hyderabad tower). Parent has mandated 8-10% cost efficiency on non-talent opex for FY26.

FY26: seat utilisation to 80%; cloud cost below 18% of total non-talent opex; contract staff ratio to 22%.
