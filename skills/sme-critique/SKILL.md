# SME Critique

Scores modeled initiatives for evidence readiness and surfaces the highest-value SME questions needed to qualify assumptions before commitment.

## Inputs

- `savings-modeler`
- `spend-profiler`
- `peer-benchmarker`
- `root-cause-analyzer`
- `contract-lifecycle-manager`

## Outputs

- Portfolio critique summary.
- Initiative-level evidence maturity.
- Top probe questions for chat CTAs and advisory follow-up.
